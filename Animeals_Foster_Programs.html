WHAT IS THE ADULT FOSTER PROGRAM?

Kitten season is the time of year when our shelter is flooded with homeless litters, starting in Spring and ending in late Fall. This high volume of babies presents many challenges for our staff and the kittens we are caring for. It is critical that these babies are placed into temporary homes because of a condition known as “failure to thrive." A kitten’s odds are greatly diminished when their first days are spent in a shelter. They NEED to be in a home. Our Foster Program doubles the amount of lives we can save. AniMeals pays for all of the food and supplies that our foster families need to ensure the survival of their tiny charges. Our Foster Program saves an average of 200 kittens annually.

While there are many families that are ready to foster kittens, there are far fewer homes willing to foster an adult cat. Shelter life can be harder on the adults as their stay with us is usually much longer than the kittens. Providing temporary relief in a home environment can be extremely beneficial for the emotional well being of these kitties while they wait for their forever homes. We provide training and support, medications if needed, food and litter, and we absorb all veterinary expenses associated with their care. Please consider becoming a foster to our adult kitties and open your heart and home to some of the most overlooked kitties in our shelter.
